Our Process

1. Surface Preparation

Thorough cleaning, sanding, and primer application ensure the new paint adheres perfectly.

2. Precision Masking

We protect surrounding panels and glass with professional-grade masking tape and paper.

3. Spray Application

Multiple thin coats are applied in a climate-controlled booth to prevent runs and orange peel.

4. Clear Coat & Polish

A UV-resistant clear coat seals the finish, followed by a meticulous polish for mirror shine.
