What is a Polyurea Hybrid?

Polyurea is a tough rubber polymer, resistant to sunlight, chemicals, and heat.

  • Durability: Superior thermal resilience and chemical resistance make it ideal for truck beds and harsh conditions.
  • Temperature Tolerance: Withstands extreme heat, humidity, and salt exposure without melting or degrading.
  • Corrosion Resistance: Resists acid, alkali, and environmental damage even at extreme temperatures.
  • Versatile Applications: From truck beds to bumpers, flat decks, and even boat trailers.
