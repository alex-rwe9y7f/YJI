Our unique rubbery texture ensures your cargo stays put, preventing sliding and protecting your truck bed from wear, tear, and scratches. This non-slip surface is so effective, we've been trusted to spray everything from Zodiac interiors to tow trucks.

UV Stabilized & Fade Resistant

Unlike other liners that fade in a few months, Yellow Jacket Liners have color infused directly into the polyurea hybrid material. This means the color stays crisp and true, keeping your truck bed looking brand new for years to come.

Lifetime Warranty

Our bed liners are made from a durable two-compound polyurethane/polyuria hybrid coating that is built to last. We are so confident in our product that we offer a lifetime warranty—you’ll never need to replace your bed liner as long as you own the vehicle!

Quick & Safe Application

Using a high-volume, high-pressure Graco spray system, our liners cure in seconds. This means you can drive your vehicle away the same day with no off-gassing, ensuring a safe and convenient experience.

What is a polyurea hybrid?

Polyurea is made of rubber polymers that are durable, resistant to wear and tear, and are resistant to sunlight, chemicals, and heat.

  • Durability: Polyurea is an extremely tough material with a superior level of thermal resilience, which makes it perfect for tough working conditions such as truck beds. It’s also chemical and water resistant.
  • Temperature tolerance: Polyurea coatings are designed to withstand heat and are therefore water and chemical resistant, even under extreme temperatures. It will not melt, or expand, when exposed to fire or high humidity. It also provides excellent resistance to salt spray, salt clouds, ice, snow, and moisture vapor.
  • Corrosion and rust resistance: Polyurea spray-coated metal components have been found to resist corrosion from acid and alkali withstanding temperatures of – 500 degree Fahrenheit.. This feature makes polyurea the ideal polyurea liner for bed liners.
  • Applications: Bumpers and side skirts to enhance overall vehicle safety and reduce the overall frequency of vehicle damage and accident occurrence. Truck beds, vehicle interiors, flat decks, entire vehicles, boat trailers.
